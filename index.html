基于Max/MSP的算法音乐 / Algorithmic Music with Max/MSP

《脉搏的拓片》 / "Pulse Rubbing"

Max/MSP

这是一个探索声音原生性与再生的实时音频生成系统。我们将一个采样音色，解构为不同频率、音调与时间形态（点与线）的切片，并将这些切片"记忆"为全新的音符单元，共同构成一件自衍生的乐器。系统驱动这件乐器内部的音符，以多重速度与随机性进行组合、对话与层叠，每一次演算，都是对原始声音"脉搏"的一次不可复制的"拓印"，生成流动而有机的乐曲。作品在实验性的技术逻辑之下，探讨声音的痕迹、变异与自主生命，邀请听者感受一场声音挣脱载体后，在数字场域中自由生长的哲学叙事。

This is a real-time audio generation system exploring the primitiveness and regeneration of sound. We deconstruct a sampled timbre into slices of different frequencies, pitches, and temporal forms (points and lines), "memorizing" these slices as new note units that together form a self-generating instrument. The system drives the notes within this instrument, combining, dialoguing, and layering them with multiple speeds and randomness. Each computation is an unrepeatable "rubbing" of the original sound's "pulse," generating fluid and organic music. Beneath its experimental technical logic, the work explores the traces, mutations, and autonomous life of sound, inviting listeners to experience a philosophical narrative of sound breaking free from its carrier and growing freely in the digital realm.

《熵的预言》 / "Prophecy of Entropy"

Max/MSP
Touch Designer

这是一件聚焦"机器自主运动"未来叙事的实验性作品。视觉上，以TD粒子与持续分叉的动态线条构建充满张力、不确定性的黑白场景，隐喻随机与叙事路径的纠缠；音效融合loop音色、点状高频、低频鼓及自然采样，经五轨随机演奏（一轨pad基底+四轨随机音轨）驱动视觉叙事。作品借机器脱离人类操控的预言意象，叩问人类在熵增时代的方向与存在意义，以视听随机生成的混沌美感，呈现科技与人文的思辨性对话。

This is an experimental work focusing on the future narrative of "autonomous machine movement." Visually, it constructs tense, uncertain black-and-white scenes with TD particles and continuously bifurcating dynamic lines, metaphorizing the entanglement of randomness and narrative paths. The sound design blends loop timbres, pointillistic high frequencies, low-frequency drums, and natural samples, driven by five tracks of random performance (one pad base + four random tracks) to propel the visual narrative. Through the prophetic imagery of machines breaking free from human control, the work questions humanity's direction and existential meaning in the age of entropy increase, presenting a contemplative dialogue between technology and humanities through the chaotic beauty of audiovisual random generation.

基于Cubase的电子音乐 / Electronic Music with Cubase

《对话的维度》 / "Dimensions of Dialogue"

Cubase

这是一首叙事性电子音乐作品。他从人的自身出发，探讨与自然界的对话以及共存。因共存而对话，又因对话而共存，使其达到人与自然和谐共生的境界。整部电子音乐主要运用shevannai omnisphere kontakt音源，力求构建一个富有哲思的音乐空间。

This is a narrative electronic music work. Starting from the human self, it explores dialogue and coexistence with the natural world. Coexistence leads to dialogue, and dialogue leads to coexistence, achieving a state of harmonious symbiosis between humans and nature. The entire electronic music primarily uses shevannai omnisphere kontakt sound sources, striving to construct a philosophical musical space.

行为与数字媒介交互 / Performance & Digital Media Interaction

《乐园·浮世众生》 / "Paradise: Floating World Beings"

Max/MSP
Kinect
Cubase

《乐园·浮世众生》是基于Kinect动作捕捉的电子音乐舞台演出。作品将地球比作人类赖以生存的乐园，探讨万物对人类的敞开与包容。敦煌壁画作为视觉核心，以其极高的艺术价值呈现人类对理想世界的想象。通过Kinect实时捕捉舞者动作，驱动AE生成的视觉内容，结合Cubase创作的融合远古人声与中国鼓等音源的音乐，构建出一场探索人与自然和谐共生、传统文化与数字媒介对话的多维艺术体验。

"Paradise: Floating World Beings" is an electronic music stage performance based on Kinect motion capture. The work compares Earth to a paradise where humans depend for survival, exploring how all things open and accommodate humanity. Dunhuang murals serve as the visual core, presenting humanity's imagination of an ideal world with their极高 artistic value. Through real-time Kinect capture of dancers' movements driving AE-generated visual content, combined with music created in Cubase fusing ancient human voices, Chinese drums, and other sound sources, the work constructs a multidimensional artistic experience exploring harmonious coexistence between humans and nature, and dialogue between traditional culture and digital media.

装置作品 / Installation Works

《渡》 / "Crossing"

AE
投影
雾机
灯光

《渡》是一个探讨水之哲学意象的多媒体装置作品。水在唐诗中承载着丰富的情感与哲思，既是时间流逝的隐喻，也是心境变迁的写照。作品运用投影、灯光与雾机的交织，在空间中再现水的流动、蒸腾与凝结，营造出东方哲学中"上善若水"的意境。雾气弥漫中，光影与水汽共舞，引导观者沉思生命如水的流动性与转化，在虚实之间体验一场关于存在、变迁与超越的冥想之旅。

"Crossing" is a multimedia installation exploring the philosophical imagery of water. In Tang poetry, water carries rich emotions and philosophies, serving as a metaphor for the passage of time and a reflection of changing states of mind. The work uses the interplay of projection, lighting, and fog machines to recreate the flow, evaporation, and condensation of water in space, evoking the Eastern concept of "the highest good is like water." In the mist-filled environment, light and water vapor dance together, guiding viewers to contemplate the fluidity and transformation of life like water, experiencing a meditative journey about existence, change, and transcendence between reality and illusion.
